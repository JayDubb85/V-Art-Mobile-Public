Support

How can we help?

Use GitHub Issues for bug reports and feature requests. Email support is available if you do not want to post publicly.

Open a GitHub Issue Email Support

Microphone

V-Art asks for microphone permission only when you tap to listen. To change permission later, open iOS Settings, choose V-Art, and update Microphone access.

Recognition

Song recognition requires music playing clearly near the device and a working network connection for ShazamKit catalog matching.

Availability

Lyrics, artist details, and concert listings depend on third-party services and may not be available for every song or artist.

Local App Data

Recognized song metadata may be stored locally so the app can reuse details when possible. Deleting the app removes local app data.

Need privacy details? Read the V-Art Mobile Privacy Policy.
